A Legacy Founded on Trust

Venus Group was founded in 1997 in Muvattupuzha by three brothers— Muhammed C S, Haris C S and Saheer C S. What began as a single manufacturing and distribution unit has today evolved into a multi-vertical business group serving B2B and B2C markets across Kerala.

The foundation of our growth has always been built on:

  • • Customer trust
  • • Honest and transparent dealings
  • • Consistent delivery systems
  • • Long-term trade relationships
