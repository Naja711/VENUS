Our Story

Building a legacy of trust and quality in Kerala since 1997.

The Beginning

Roots in Muvattupuzha

Venus Group was established in 1997 in Muvattupuzha by brothers Muhammed C S, Haris C S, and Saheer C S.

The name “Venus” carries legacy- inspired by the tobacco firm and the locally known Venus Hotel founded by their late father. The name symbolized familiarity, goodwill, and trust in the community- values that continue to guide the group today.

From its first firm in 1997, the group has steadily expanded across distribution, retail, and trade sectors while maintaining a reputation for transparent business practices and dependable service.
